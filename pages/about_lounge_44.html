Our Story

Where contemporary sophistication meets warm Ugandan hospitality in the heart of Kyengera

The Vision Behind Lounge 44

Founded in 2023 by hospitality visionary Sarah Nakamya, Lounge 44 emerged from a simple yet profound observation: Kyengera deserved a destination that honored both international sophistication and authentic Ugandan warmth.

After years of experience in Kampala's premium hospitality scene, Sarah recognized the opportunity to create something extraordinary in Kyengera's evolving social landscape—a place where exceptional cuisine meets captivating entertainment, where business meetings seamlessly transition into evening celebrations.

"Every visit should feel like a celebration," Sarah explains. "We're not just serving food or hosting events—we're creating memories and fostering genuine connections within our community."
